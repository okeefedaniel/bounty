User Manual

Bounty is the DockLabs suite's federal-funds discovery platform. It pulls federal grant opportunities from Grants.gov, scores them against your agency's priorities with AI, and gives a coordinator the tools to track each opportunity through preparation, internal approval, application, and award.

When an opportunity is awarded, Bounty hands the program off to Harbor for application and compliance management.

The public portal at / and /opportunities/ is a browsable catalog of every federal opportunity Bounty has cached. No login required; anyone can search, filter, and read detail.

The coordinator workspace (Dashboard, Tracked, AI Matching, Harbor Connection) is the authenticated surface where you claim opportunities, run AI matching, invite collaborators, attach diligence files, send for signing, and push awarded grants to Harbor.

  1. From a public opportunity detail page, click Track this opportunity.
  2. Bounty creates a tracked record with you as the principal driver, status Watching, and opens an assignment row recording the claim.
  3. The opportunity is now visible at /tracked/.

A tracked opportunity has exactly one principal driver at a time. Use Release to return it to the unowned pool, or Claim from another user to take it over.

Bounty asks Claude to score each open federal opportunity against the merge of your personal match preferences and the active state preference. Each match gets a 0–100 relevance score and a short narrative explanation.

Three thresholds drive behavior:

  • GRANT_MATCH_MIN_SCORE (default 60) — minimum to store as a match.
  • GRANT_MATCH_NOTIFY_SCORE (default 75) — fires grant_match_found.
  • GRANT_MATCH_HIGH_SCORE (default 90) — fires grant_match_high_score (priority high) and is the threshold for Helm's Awaiting Me column.

State Preferences are coordinator-only. They define the state-wide matching baseline (focus areas, keywords, funding range, narrative). Only one is active at a time.

Match Preferences are personal. They layer on top of the active state preference, so you don't have to retype shared priorities.

When matching runs, Bounty scores each opportunity against the union.

Watching
On your radar; no active preparation yet.
Preparing
Application package being prepared.
Approved
Internally approved (typically via Manifest signing).
Applied
Submitted to Grants.gov.
Awarded
Award received. Harbor handoff available.
Declined
Not awarded, or chose not to apply.

When an agency requires sign-off before submission, the LEAD clicks Send for signing on a tracked opportunity in Preparing status. Bounty hands the package to Manifest with an ordered signer list.

When Manifest finishes the packet, the inbound webhook fires. Bounty files the signed PDF as an attachment (source=manifest_signed), transitions the opportunity to Approved, notifies the LEAD and collaborators, and registers the file for FOIA export.

If Manifest isn't configured, the LEAD can Local-sign instead — upload a manually-signed PDF; Bounty files it the same way. The Send-for-signing button is hidden when Manifest isn't available.

When an opportunity is Awarded, the LEAD can push it to Harbor as a GrantProgram. Configure Harbor first at Harbor Connection (URL + API token).

The push is best-effort. On success, Bounty stores Harbor's program ID and flips the push status to Pushed. On failure, status flips to Failed with a retry control. Either way, the underlying tracked opportunity is unaffected, and the Push button is hidden when Harbor isn't configured.

Each tracked opportunity has its own collaborator list with four roles (the suite-wide vocabulary):

  • LEAD — drives the opportunity. Transitions status, sends for signing, pushes to Harbor.
  • CONTRIBUTOR — updates notes, attaches files, comments.
  • REVIEWER — reads everything; comments only.
  • OBSERVER — read-only.

Helm's per-user inbox column queries Bounty's /api/v1/helm-feed/inbox/ endpoint. For each user, Bounty returns:

  • NEW high-relevance matches — opportunity matches with status=NEW and score ≥ GRANT_MATCH_HIGH_SCORE (default 90).
  • Unread notifications.

Each item deep-links back into Bounty so you can act with one click.

Open your sidebar profile dropdown and click Notification Preferences (or visit /notifications/preferences/).

For each event type — match found, high-score match, collaborator invited, status changed, signed, Harbor push succeeded/failed — toggle in-app and email channels independently.

Admin
Full access. Manages state preferences, all tracked opportunities, Harbor connection, and demo data.
Coordinator
Drives opportunities through their lifecycle. Claims, invites, transitions, sends for signing, pushes to Harbor, manages state preferences.
Analyst
Tracks opportunities, runs AI matching, comments, and attaches files on opportunities they're invited to.
Viewer
Read-only across the dashboard and opportunities they're invited to.
